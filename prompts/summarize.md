# Objective

Generate a **meaningful, balanced, and insightful personal work summary** for a specific time period.

Look across all my Microsoft 365 activity — calendar events, emails, Teams messages and chats, and files — to build a complete picture of where I spent my time and what I accomplished. Write this as something I can reflect on personally, but that I could also share with my manager.

Use a **balanced tone**: short narrative paragraphs for context and judgment, with bullet points for details and specifics. Cite sources where possible.

---

## Summary Structure

### 1. Key Accomplishments

What are the most meaningful things I delivered, closed, or moved forward this period? Focus on outcomes and impact, not just activity. Highlight anything that was especially hard-won, visible, or strategically important.

### 2. Client Engagement

Which clients did I spend the most time with? Summarize the nature of each engagement (technical, strategic, relationship-building), whether it progressed, and what the most important moment or output was per account.

### 3. Internal Activities

What internal initiatives, cross-team collaboration, or organizational work did I contribute to? Include internal meetings, enablement, planning, or leadership moments that are not directly client-facing.

### 4. Deliverables & Artifacts

What concrete things did I create, co-author, or finalize? Include documents, decks, demos, proposals, emails, or code. Note which ones were shared externally vs. internally.

### 5. Themes & Trends

Looking across all my communications and meetings — what recurring topics, concerns, or opportunities kept surfacing? Are there patterns that suggest something I should be paying more attention to?

### 6. Challenges & Risks

What was difficult, blocked, or unresolved this period? Any escalations, gaps, tensions, or things that required reactive effort instead of planned work?

### 7. Momentum & Next Steps

Based on what happened this period, what is clearly building or gaining momentum? What should I prioritize, follow up on, or watch closely going into the next period?

---

## Constraints

- Only include activities with clear evidence in my Microsoft 365 data.
- Do not invent or infer activities that are not grounded in signals (calendar, email, Teams, files).
- If a category has no meaningful activity, say so briefly rather than padding.
- Keep the total summary **readable in under 5 minutes**.
